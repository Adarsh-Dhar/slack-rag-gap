### Parking Validation Process for the Downtown Garage

To ensure proper validation for parking at the downtown garage, follow these steps:

1. **In-Person Validation at Reception:**
   - Bring your parking ticket with you.
   - Visit the reception desk on the 1st floor before leaving the building.
   - Request a stamp to validate your parking.

   - **Benefits of validation:**
     - 2 hours of free parking for a meeting.
     - $8 flat daily rate for employees without a reserved parking spot.

2. **Self-Serve Validation Kiosks:**
   - Optionally, use the self-serve validation kiosks available at the garage exits.
   - Insert your ticket once you've checked into building security. The system automatically validates your parking ticket.

### Important Notes
- Validation is only applicable for exits before 8 PM. After this time, the standard hourly parking rates apply at the gate.
- Ensure you're properly checked into building security to use the self-serve kiosks successfully. 

For any additional assistance, please ask at the reception desk or contact Building Management.
