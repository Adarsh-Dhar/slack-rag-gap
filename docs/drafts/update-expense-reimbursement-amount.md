# Company Handbook (Sample)

## Vacation Policy

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly at 1.25 days/month.

Vacation requests must be submitted in Slack via the #time-off channel at least 5 business days in advance for approval by your manager. Unused vacation days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees may submit expense reports for approved business expenses including client meals (up to $50 per person), travel, and necessary software subscriptions. Reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense with itemized receipts attached. Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core collaboration hours are 10am-3pm in your local timezone. Remote work requests for more than 3 days per week require manager approval and are reviewed quarterly.
