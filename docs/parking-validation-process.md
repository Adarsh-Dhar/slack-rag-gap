### Parking Validation Steps

1. **Locate the kiosk**: The validation kiosk is situated near the elevator on the P2 level of the downtown garage.

2. **Scan your garage ticket**: Insert or scan the ticket you received when entering the garage at the kiosk.

3. **Tap your meeting badge**: After scanning your ticket, tap your meeting badge to complete the validation process. This will reduce the parking rate to a flat $5 for the first 4 hours.

4. **Alternative validation method**: If the kiosk is not operational, visit the front desk. Provide your visitor badge number, and they can manually validate your parking.

### Notes:
- This process only applies to visitors attending meetings and requires a valid meeting badge.
- Ensure you validate your ticket before exiting the garage to avoid higher parking fees.
